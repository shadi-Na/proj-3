Our product specifications

The 2022 Kia Rio is powered by a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 120 horsepower and 151 Nm of torque. The engine is mated to a continuously variable transmission with electronic precision shifting.

The car is available with 18-inch rims that raise the stability of the car on roads as well as the aesthetic touch. Providing mirrors through which to determine the blind spot. The presence of 2 shakman exits and the presence of inverters. Providing rear sensors to assist in reversing after collisions.

It features many entertainment and convenience features such as a touch screen with Apple Carplay and Android Auto, and separate rear seats.
